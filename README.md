# 🧬 BioMaster — IGCSE Biology MCQ Practice App

Master your Cambridge IGCSE Biology (0610) exams with **BioMaster**, a modern, interactive, and fully-featured practice suite. Packed with **230 exam-style multiple-choice questions** split into two comprehensive parts, it provides all the tools you need to achieve an A*.

- **Part 1 (Core Syllabus)**: 130 foundational questions spanning all major topics.
- **Part 2 (Extended & High-Yield)**: 100 new, challenging questions targeting advanced concepts and recent exam trends.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **📚 Topic-by-Topic Study**: Master individual chapters at your own pace. Your progress is automatically saved to your browser's local storage.
- **🧪 Full-Length Mock Tests**: Simulate the real Cambridge Paper 1. Choose your question count (10, 20, or 40 questions) and set a timer (including the official 45-minute limit).
- **⚡ Weak Areas Dashboard**: The app automatically identifies topics where your score is below 70%, helping you focus on the areas that need the most improvement.
- **🪤 Examiner Traps Guide**: Learn to identify and avoid common tricks, tricky phrasing, and keyword traps that Cambridge examiners frequently use to catch students out.
- **🔥 Gamified Streak Tracker**: Keep your momentum going with a real-time correct answer streak indicator and congratulatory confetti.
- **🎨 Premium Responsive UI**: Designed with a state-of-the-art dark theme, animated background glassmorphism orbs, particle grids, and smooth animations.

## 📖 Syllabus Topics Covered

1. **Cell Biology** (Organelles, organisation, magnification calculations)
2. **Classification** (Living characteristics, vertebrates/invertebrates, dichotomous keys)
3. **Movement In & Out of Cells** (Diffusion, osmosis, active transport, water potential)
4. **Biological Molecules** (Food tests, element identification, nutrient energy)
5. **Enzymes** (Lock-and-key model, optimum temperature/pH, denaturation)
6. **Plant Nutrition** (Photosynthesis equations, limiting factors, leaf structure)
7. **Human Nutrition** (Digestive system, enzymes, absorption, villi adaptations)
8. **Transport in Plants** (Xylem & phloem, transpiration, translocation)
9. **Transport in Animals** (Heart structure, double circulation, vessel comparison)
10. **Diseases & Immunity** (Pathogens, active/passive immunity, vaccines)
11. **Gas Exchange** (Alveoli adaptations, ventilation mechanics, smoking impacts)
12. **Respiration** (Aerobic vs. anaerobic, yeast fermentation, lactic acid)
13. **Excretion** (Deamination, kidney filtration, urea excretion)
14. **Coordination & Response** (Reflex arc, eye accommodation, pupil reflex, hormones)
15. **Drugs** (Antibiotic actions and limitations)
16. **Reproduction** (Mitosis vs. meiosis, gametes, pollination, fertilization)
17. **Inheritance** (Monohybrid crosses, genotype/phenotype, pedigree charts)
18. **Variation & Selection** (Continuous/discontinuous, natural/artificial selection)
19. **Ecology & Human Influences** (Food chains, trophic levels, eutrophication)
20. **Biotechnology & Genetic Engineering** (Yeast/pectinase use, plasmid vectors)
